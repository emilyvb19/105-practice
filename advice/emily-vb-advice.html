Hi, my name is Emily and here's some advice for all you new coders!

Keep at it!

Coding is something that can frustrate you a lot! But don't worry, as long as you keep at it and don't give up, you'll get it!

And it's the best feeling when you figure it out!

Practice, practice, practice!

Practice will be your friend, trust me. Keep working on your classes and maybe even throw in your own little project! It will only benefit you down the road.

Schedule time everyday to work towards those coding goals, even if it's just an hour. It will keep you on your toes and you won't have time to forget anything.

Be patient and kind to yourself, you're learning something new after all!
